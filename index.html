What we are about

Civic engagemant amoung young people in Tanzania has been on a decline. We are about to CHANGE that. Though our kijana Voice platform and #NguvuYaTano campaign we are influence young people to register network, run for office, observe, follow up and speak up

Who we are for

We are determined to get as many young people as possible into voting stations ready to choose youth candidates or older candidates who have viable youth agendas in their plans.

To achieve this, we are dedicated to make civic and voting education 'youthfully accessible', assist and inspiring young candidates to run for office and empower young journalists to report on election processes.

Why we do it

Our democracy is at stake because the distance between youth and political institutions is growing exponentially and youth political apathy is at a rise. We want to make voting, politics and civic discussions fun again by absorbing them into the modern Tanzanian youth culture.
